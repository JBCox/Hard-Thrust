## Issues Found

### MAJOR (Should Fix)

#### 1. Filter Word Density: "felt/feel/feeling"
**Count:** 203 instances
**Impact:** Creates narrative distance, filters reader from experience
**Target:** Reduce by 50%+ (to ~100)

**Pattern to find:**
```
\bfelt\b|\bfeel\b|\bfeeling\b
```

**Fix approach:**
- "She felt cold" → "Cold seeped through her jacket"
- "He felt afraid" → "Fear tightened his chest"
- "I felt like" → Delete or rewrite with action

#### 2. Filter Word Density: "saw/see/seeing"
**Count:** 247 instances
**Impact:** Filters reader from direct experience
**Target:** Reduce by 40%+ (to ~150)

**Pattern to find:**
```
\bsaw\b|\bsee\b|\bseeing\b
```

**Fix approach:**
- "He saw the door open" → "The door swung open"
- "She saw him approach" → "He approached"
- "I could see" → Delete entirely
