#### "Something" Analysis (257 occurrences)

Still overused but improved. Target reduction to ~200.

**Pattern types:**
- "something about [noun]" - vague attribution
- "something was wrong" - could name the wrongness
- "felt something" - could name the emotion

**Example fixes:**
- "Something about her expression..." → "Her expression..."
- "I felt something shift" → "The tension shifted"

#### "Just" Analysis (288 occurrences)

Often functions as filler. Target reduction to ~200.

**Example fixes:**
- "I just stood there" → "I stood there"
- "just a thought" → "a thought"
