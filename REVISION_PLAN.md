## AUDIT METHODOLOGY

When checking for issues, always verify:

1. **WHO is the POV character?** Rules differ for Josh vs Eleanor chapters.

2. **WHAT chapter/act is this?** Check the date header. Use this timeline:
   - Act 1: October 22 - November 15 (setup, no DID hints)
   - Act 2: November 16 - February 27 (escalation, misdirection OK)
   - Act 3: February 28+ (reveals, full truth OK)

3. **IS IT DISMISSED?** A character having a suspicious thought and then rationalizing it away is MISDIRECTION, not a spoiler.

4. **DOES THE MATH CHECK OUT?** Before flagging timeline issues:
   - Josh born ~1995 (age 3 in 1998) → age 29 in 2024 ✓
   - Eleanor at Thornewood April 2024 → 6 months before October ✓
   - Day counts from October 22 arrival

5. **WHAT'S THE NARRATIVE PURPOSE?** Ask: does this serve the misdirection (readers suspect Eleanor) or does it spoil the twist (readers suspect Josh)?
